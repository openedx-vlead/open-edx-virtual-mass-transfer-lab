BEFORE EXPERIMENT

Outer diameter of the benzoic acid cylinder = 3.4 cm

Length of the benzoic acid cylinder = 4.9 cm

AFTER EXPERIMENT

Outer diameter of the benzoic acid cylinder = 2.75 cm

Length of the benzoic acid cylinder = 4.01 cm

Normality of HCL used for titration (N1) = 0.5 N

Volume of 0.5 N HCL used (V1) = 9.4 ml

Volume of NaOH taken (V2) = 10 ml

Let the normality of unused NaOH in dissolution be N2

N1V1 = N2V2

N2 =N1V1 /V2

=0.47 N

The concentration of unused NaOH = 0.47 N

The rate of dissolution of benzoic acid = R

R = N2*Volume of NaOH soln in tank

=1.175 *10^(-6) kmol/sec

Let the average dimensions of benzoic acid cylinder be

Dav = (3.4 + 2.75 )/2

= 3.075 cm

Lav = (4.9 + 4.01)/2

= 4.455 cm
